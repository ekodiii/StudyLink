How to Use StudyLink

1

Install the Chrome Extension

Install the StudyLink Chrome Extension to sync your Canvas assignments.

Important: After installing, reload your Canvas page for the extension to start syncing.

2

Sign in to the Extension

Click the StudyLink extension icon and sign in with Google or Apple using the same account you used here.

3

Sync Your Assignments

Visit any Canvas course page and the extension will automatically sync your assignments. You can also click "Sync Now" in the extension popup.

4

Create or Join a Group

Create a study group and share the invite code, or join an existing group with a code from your classmates.

5

Track Progress Together

See at a glance which assignments you and your study group have completed. Assignments automatically update when you submit them in Canvas.

6

Manage Course Visibility

Use the Courses tab to hide courses site-wide, or the Visibility tab to control which courses each group can see.
